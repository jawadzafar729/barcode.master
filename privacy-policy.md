1. Introduction
BarcodeMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is a barcode and QR code scanning application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application. By using BarcodeMaster, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect
2.1 Information You Provide
We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use BarcodeMaster.

2.2 Camera Data
BarcodeMaster uses your device's camera solely to scan barcodes and QR codes in real time. Camera images and video frames are:

Processed entirely on your device
Never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any external server by us
Discarded immediately after scanning is complete
2.3 Scan History (Local Storage)
Scanned barcode results (e.g., text, URLs, contact details) may be saved to a local database on your device (Room/SQLite) so you can review your scan history. This data:

Never leaves your device
Can be cleared at any time from within the app
Is not accessible to us or any third party
2.4 Automatically Collected Data
We do not directly collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics. However, third-party SDKs integrated into the app may collect certain non-personal data as described in Section 4.
